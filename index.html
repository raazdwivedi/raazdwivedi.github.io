Raaz RSK Dwivedi

 

  • Currently: Visiting Assistant Professor, Cornell ORIE

  • Starting 2024: Assistant Professor, Cornell ORIE, Cornell Tech

  • Research: Fast and effective strategies for personalized decision-making with ideas from causal inference, reinforcement learning, and distribution compression with applications in healthcare

  • 2021-2023: FODSI Postdoctoral Fellow, Harvard & MIT, co-advised by Prof. Susan Murphy & Prof. Devavrat Shah

  • 2015-2021: Ph.D., EECS, UC Berkeley, co-advised by Prof. Martin Wainwright & Prof. Bin Yu (thesis)

  • 2010-2014: B. Tech., EE, IIT Bombay, advised by Prof. Vivek Borkar (thesis)

  • Contact: Rhodes 226 Ithaca, dwivedi@cornell.edu, Google Scholar
