My research interests span both the theoretical and applied statistical aspects of machine learning (ML) and data science (DS). Together with several excellent collaborators, my past and ongoing works develop novel methods, and theory for challenging problems in high-dimensional statistics, Bayesian inference, and causal inference. I finished my Ph.D. at UC Berkeley in August 2021 with thesis titled ‘‘Principled Statistical Approaches For Sampling and Inference in High Dimensions’’, slides available here.
